ALL 125 Argumentation Presentation Preparation

Helping students prepare for the ALL 125 Argumentation Presentation assignment. Navigate through the sections to plan your presentation.

Topic Finalisation

Teams must finalise their chosen topic. Focus on the theme, identify the problem, and specify the argument you're addressing. Write your team's chosen topic and decisions below.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.

Thesis Finalisation

State your team's clear position on the chosen topic. Ensure you've identified the problem and specified the argument you're addressing. Write your thesis statement here.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.

Thesis Justification

List 2-3 main reasons supporting your thesis. Focus on how these reasons address the problem you've identified. Write your main reasons here.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.

Data Identification

Identify qualitative and quantitative data that supports your argument. Ensure the data directly addresses the problem you've identified. List data sources and how they support your argument.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.

Team Roles

Assign specific roles to each team member. Ensure each role contributes to addressing the problem and building the argument. Examples: Leader, Researcher, Visual Designer, Translator, AI Image Generator, Presenter.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.

Steps to be Taken

Outline the specific tasks and deadlines for each team member. Ensure each task contributes to addressing the problem and building the argument. List tasks, responsible members, and deadlines.

Your notes will be saved as a .txt file on your device.
